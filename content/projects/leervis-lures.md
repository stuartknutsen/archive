Leervis (garrick) chase a lure that moves like something fleeing. The
subsurface walker is shaped to slide side-to-side on a slack-line twitch;
the copper spoon is the simpler, older answer that caught the first fish.

Everything here is made by hand and tested in the water, which is the only
review that counts.
