My name is Stuart Knutsen and I am studying electrical and electronic engineering at the University of Cape Town. What's here is everything I make and photograph, inside and outside of university work.

I like to take the time to fully understand how something works and the inside details rather than black boxing it.

This is an archive, not a portfolio: everything goes, whether it is finished and polished or a complete failure. You can learn a lot by questioning why something has failed.
